Here are some Dos and Don'ts for password creation:

Dos:

Make your password a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols (like @, #, or !).

Try to make your password at least 8 characters long. The longer, the better!

Choose a password that no one else can guess. Don’t use your name or birthday.

Don'ts:

Don’t use the same password everywhere, if one site gets hacked, you don’t want all your accounts to be in danger.

Never tell your password to anyone, not even your best friend or sibling.

Avoid using things like your phone number, school name, or family members' names in your password.

EXAMPLE:

Bad Password: soccer1

Good Password: $0cc3rP1ay3r
